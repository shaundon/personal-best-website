## What is Personal Best?

Personal Best is a workout tracker for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. It helps you plan and track workouts, and gives you all sorts of insights afterwards.

### 🩷 Powered by Apple Health (a.k.a. HealthKit)
Works with the apps you already use to record workouts. Zero setup, just connect to Apple Health and you're all set.

### 🎯 Goals and coaching
Set goals like _3 workouts every week_ or _4 hours of cycling every week_ and Personal Best will coach you, with tracking, reminders, and suggested workouts. Plan workouts ahead of time like "a 5km run today" and it'll track how you did compared to what you committed to.

### 📹 Replay
Scrub through any workout like a video, with an animated route map, pace and heart rate charts, and an integrated photo gallery from the moments you captured along the way. [See a demo &raquo;](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/Xc3eh3BBI00)

### 🌃 Sharing
Turn your workouts into images for social media, with editorial-quality design and customisation.

### 📊 Go deep on stats
Leaderboards so you can track your progress. See your calorie burn compared to foods, your distance to well-known routes, and your elevation to famous landmarks. If a stat exists, it's probably in Personal Best.

### 🌍 Works everywhere
Personal Best works on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. It comes with lots of widgets and complications for keeping score when the app isn't running.

### 🫂 Respects users
Minimal tracking. No ads. Good accessibility.

## What's the story behind it?

I’m [Shaun](https://mstdn.social/@shaundon), an indie developer from London, UK. I started Personal Best as a small hobby project, and I enjoyed it so much that I just carried on building it. It's now had over 500,000 downloads and has an active and passionate user base.

It's regularly featured by Apple, both on the App Store and also at developer events as an example of best practice.
